# AnonymousChat: Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Ethos
AnonymousChat exists to provide secure, unmonitored communication without sacrificing usability. We believe that privacy is a fundamental human right, not a premium feature. 

**Voice & Tone:**
- **Clear:** We do not use jargon. We explain complex cryptography in simple terms.
- **Spartan:** We use as few words as necessary. We respect the user's time.
- **Informative:** We tell the user exactly what is happening to their data.
- *Rule:* Avoid buzzwords (e.g., "synergy", "blockchain", "metaverse"). Do not use em-dashes.

## 2. Color Palette
Our color palette is inspired by modern tech giants (Apple, Stripe, Claude) to evoke trust, security, and premium quality.

### Primary Colors
- **iOS Blue (Primary):** `#0A84FF`
- **Deep Indigo (Secondary):** `#5E5CE6`
- **Surface Black (Background):** `#000000`

### Utility Colors
- **Success Green:** `#32D74B`
- **Crimson Error:** `#FF453A`
- **Warning Orange:** `#FF9F0A`

### Neutrals
- **Text Primary:** `#F2F2F7`
- **Text Secondary:** `#AEAEB2`

## 3. Typography
**Primary Font Family:** `Inter`, fallback to system sans-serif (`-apple-system`, `BlinkMacSystemFont`, `Segoe UI`).

- **H1 (Logo & Major Headers):** 24px, Font Weight 800 (ExtraBold), Letter Spacing -1px.
- **H2 (Section Titles):** 20px, Font Weight 600 (SemiBold).
- **Body Text:** 15px, Font Weight 400 (Regular), Line Height 1.5.
- **Small Text (Labels/Footers):** 12px, Font Weight 500 (Medium).

## 4. Visual Elements (Glassmorphism)
We utilize Glassmorphism to create a sense of depth and hierarchy without relying on solid, heavy borders.

- **Background:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.05)`
- **Blur Amount:** `blur(16px)`
- **Border:** `1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)`
- **Drop Shadow:** `0 8px 32px 0 rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.3)`

## 5. Logo Usage
The logo is a translucent, glassmorphic incognito emblem over an iOS Blue gradient. 
- Do not stretch or distort the logo.
- Minimum clear space around the logo must be 20% of its total width.
- Always use the provided PNGs with transparency enabled.
